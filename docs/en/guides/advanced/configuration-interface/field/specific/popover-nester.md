# Sub-form (Popover)

### Overview

For relationship tables with complex relationship levels and many fields, sub-form layout may be difficult to effectively present the primary and secondary relationships of data. In this case, sub-form (popover) displays relationship field sub-forms in popup form, providing a clearer presentation.

### Usage Instructions

#### Use in Sub-table
Embed sub-form through sub-table, supporting complex relationship data display.
![](../../../../../../public/popover-nester1.png)
![](../../../../../../public/popover-nester2.png)

#### Use in Form Block
Display sub-form in popup form within form block, suitable for scenarios requiring clear presentation of data hierarchy.
